# CrazyGames 제출용 등록 정보

제출은 https://developer.crazygames.com/games 에서 게임 **하나씩** 진행.
각 게임의 ZIP(`crazygames/<이름>.zip`)을 업로드하고 아래 내용을 붙여넣으면 됨.
(CrazyGames는 글로벌 플랫폼이라 등록 문구는 영어가 기본)

## 🐎 Game 1 — dagdak-dagdak.zip

- **Title:** Dagdak Dagdak: Horse Runner
- **Category / Genre:** Arcade / Runner
- **Short description (1 line):**
  A one-tap endless runner — gallop as a cute horse and leap over obstacles!
- **Long description:**
  Dagdak Dagdak is a fast, cheerful one-tap endless runner. Play as a cute little
  horse galloping across a neon night course — tap to jump, tap again in the air to
  double-jump over walls. Every 100 meters you level up and the horse gets faster,
  with new obstacle types unlocking as you go. Try the Daily Challenge (same course
  for everyone each day) or Free Run, and chase your best distance. Simple to learn,
  hard to master. How far can you gallop?
- **Controls / Instructions:**
  - Tap / Click / Space — Jump
  - Tap again in mid-air — Double jump (go higher)
  - Avoid the red obstacles. Survive as long as you can!
- **Tags:** runner, arcade, one button, casual, high score, horse, endless, jumping, single player, mobile
- **Orientation:** Portrait & Landscape (responsive)

## 🎯 Game 2 — bing-bing.zip

- **Title:** Bing Bing: Perfect Timing
- **Category / Genre:** Casual / Skill
- **Short description (1 line):**
  Tap when the spinning needle hits the green zone — chain combos!
- **Long description:**
  Bing Bing is a pure one-tap timing game. A needle spins around a ring — tap the
  moment it crosses the green zone. Every hit makes it faster and the target smaller,
  and every few hits the whole thing flips direction to catch you off guard. One
  mistake ends the run, so how high can your combo go? Instantly understandable,
  endlessly replayable — the "just one more try" kind of game.
- **Controls / Instructions:**
  - Tap / Click / Space — when the needle is on the green zone
  - Miss the green zone and it's game over
  - Watch for the REVERSE flash — the spin flips direction!
- **Tags:** casual, arcade, one button, reaction, timing, skill, high score, endless, single player, mobile
- **Orientation:** Portrait & Landscape (responsive)
